History

At Westbehance, we pride ourselves on being much more than just a car dealership. We are a family passionate about cars, committed to providing the best buying experience to our customers. From humble beginnings to becoming a leader in the automotive industry, we have kept our dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction at the center of everything we do.

Our mission

At Westbehance, our mission is simple: to offer our customers an unparalleled car buying experience. We strive to exceed expectations in every interaction, providing personalized attention, a premium selection of vehicles, and exceptional service at all times.

Our vision

Is to be the most trusted and respected car lot in the region. We want to be recognized for our integrity, professionalism, and passion for automobiles. We envision a future where every customer feels valued and satisfied, whether they're buying their first car or upgrading to their dream vehicle. In addition, we aspire to be leaders in innovation, offering flexible financing options and high-quality vehicles.
